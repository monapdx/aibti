## 📜 Design Principles

1. **Scarcity first.** Tier 4 has to be rare enough that triggering one earns a screenshot.
2. **Positive framing.** Even "extreme scapegoater" gets rebranded as "Ultimate Delegator" — no badge shames you.
3. **Backed by theory.** Every badge is annotated inside the Skill with a rationale: why this data pattern maps to this behavior.
4. **Partial secrecy.** Tier 4 isn't listed in the README. Users only discover them by tripping them — surprise is the point.
5. **Privacy preserved.** Badge displays still honor the scan's privacy rules. No raw prompt text leaks, ever.
